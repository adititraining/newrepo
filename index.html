Derivation of Projectile Motion

1. Reality

A ball is thrown at an angle. We want to know how far and how high it will go.

2. Idealization

  • No air resistance
  • Point object
  • Flat ground
  • Uniform gravity

3. Geometric Stage

Initial velocity is resolved:

  • Horizontal: vx = v₀ cos(θ)
  • Vertical: vy = v₀ sin(θ)

4. Algebraic Stage

Using equations of motion:

  • x(t) = v₀ cos(θ) · t
  • y(t) = v₀ sin(θ) · t - (1/2) g t²
  • Trajectory: y = x tan(θ) - (g x²)/(2 v₀² cos²(θ))
